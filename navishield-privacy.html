NaviShield Privacy Policy

NaviShield is a lightweight anti-phishing tool designed to check websites for potential malicious content. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection & Storage

NaviShield does not store or log any personal data, URLs, or browsing history. When you check a link, it is processed in real-time to evaluate site safety, but no record of the link is kept by us.

2. Data Usage

Since we do not store or log URLs, we have no data to share or sell. NaviShield checks each URL against third-party APIs (e.g., Safe Browsing, AI-based phishing checks), but those requests are ephemeral and are not recorded by NaviShield.
